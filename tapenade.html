Tapenade Dip/Spread

Tapenade is an olive heavy dip that is great spread on bread,
or if you really like olive, straight from the spoon

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 can of black, pitted olives
  • 1 large Jar of green olives, stuffed or not. I don't care
  • some form of garlic, powder, minced, or bulb
  • Worcestshire sauce
  • a jar of pepperoncinis
  • Jalepenos or some varient. Fresh or pickled
  • A willingness to measure with your heart
  • If you're feeling brave: Capers
  • Some form of bread, preferably a stiff form. Bruschetta or crackers

Steps

  1. Drain can of black olives and add olives to food processor
  2. drain green olives and fill black olive can. Dump into processor, repeat a second time
  3. do the same with de-stemmed pepperoncinis one time
  4. do the same with jalepenos, destem if fresh
  5. measure garlic with your heart, but I suggest no more than two regular bulbs of garlic. Powedered or minced garlic is roughly two tablesoons, or ten, however you feel
  6. Give two good splashes of Worcestshire Sauce
  7. roughly chop with the food processor
  8. add capers you little rebel
  9. pulse food processor a few more times until you have little chunks
  10. spread that stuff on some bread and gorge yourself. once youre done eat one more tablespoon's worth. You deserve it.
